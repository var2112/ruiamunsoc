RUIAMUN

It is with great pride that we let you know that it is going to be our first ever event since the inception of our team. With committees ranging from the Lok Sabha to International Press, RUIAMUN'22 will put forth a wide range of significant matters that are real-life threats to our existence and how the real entities of our simulation would address them. The theme for the RUIAMUN'22 is Preservation of Democracy, Equality, and Civil Liberties. Undeniably, It is true that real democracy is currently beneath the covers by virtue of loopholes in our system of understanding and eventually governance. It is important for young minds; the future of tomorrow to understand that this is not how a society should operate. The mere essence of our constitution should always shine. Democracy is not a new concept to India & to most of the world. India along with the entire world has a long tradition of tolerance of different views and creeds, which is the hallmark of any true democracy. There is also considerable evidence of the widespread existence of democratic institutions in ancient times. In the Vedic period, the republics were called Gana-Rajya. These Gana-Rajyas were autonomous and were governed by an elected Gana-Mukhya. The ancient Greek City States were and are a symbol of what transparent Governance is. Decisions were taken by voting on important issues. A democratic form of government is a government which is based on the consent of the governed. It is a system in which free public opinion is the main source of law and in which the government depends upon public opinion and responds to changes in public opinion. Democracy derives its vitality from the freedom of opinion and discussion that it tolerates. In a democracy, it is believed that truth emerges from the competition of ideas.
