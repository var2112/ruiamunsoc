Ruia Republic

The Team Ruia Republic is a brand new addition to the ever-successful embodiment of the Ruia Student Council (RSA), which represents and integrates students in all types of college activities. It was established under the umbrella of RSC by our founders and co-ordinators, Viraj Samant and Prathmesh Repal on 14th July 2022. Taking into consideration our institute's solemn motto, Explore. Experience. Excel. The main goal of our team is to instil in students the spirit of being true citizens by involving them in policy formulation and piquing their interest in current and global affairs. The outstanding merit of democracy is that by making people voters and participating in public affairs, it compels people to consider public issues and form their opinion on them. One of the rationales of democracy is that in it everybody gets a chance to be heard. All citizens have the right to express their opinions freely and thereby contribute to the right decisions and passing of good laws that govern the country. In order to actively and meaningfully participate in the democratic functioning of our civic and political institutions, citizens are required to have certain competencies. In these modern times, it is of the utmost importance for citizens, especially the rising youth of the country to be aware of all the happenings around the society. What better can we think of than politics? We believe platforms like MUNs and Youth Parliaments are an ideal place to hone public speaking, leadership, team building and analytical skills while gaining a colossal amount of knowledge during comprehensive research followed by critical analysis prior to the conference. In this new era of globalisation wherein, it is a healthier, wealthier, and more interconnected world built on the foundation of innovative technologies with numerous consequences of confusion and chaos being globally aware has become more important than ever as we reckon that such conference is the way to gain knowledge and become aware of the myriad of issues and to deliberate over their solutions during the course of such conferences. The composition, powers and functions of such conferences are generally included in the course of study at the middle, secondary and higher secondary stages of schooling. Knowledge of its procedure helps in developing an insight into the working of parliament and therefore the session of the House has special importance in developing such an insight in the young students.
