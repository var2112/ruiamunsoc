Ramnarain Ruia Autonomous College, Matunga

Ramnarain Ruia College, founded by S. P. Mandali. The National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) granted the college a "A+" GRADE and a CGPA of 3.70 for the academic year 2017-18. Our illustrious alumni include those who have achieve accomplishments in a variety of professions, including acting, business, law, research, and politics, among others.

About Team Ruia Republic

The Team Ruia Republic is a brand new addition to the ever-successful embodiment of the Ruia Student Council, which represents and integrates students in all types of college activities. Taking into consideration our institute's solemn motto, Explore. Experience. Excel. The main goal of our team is to instil in students the spirit of being true citizens by involving them in policy formulation and piquing their interest in current and global affairs.
